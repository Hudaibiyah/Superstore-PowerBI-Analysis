Superstore Sales Dashboard - Power BI Analysis

📊 Project Overview

This project involves a comprehensive analysis of the US Superstore dataset using Power BI. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive executive dashboard that identifies key performance indicators (KPIs), regional trends, and product performance.

🚀 Key Features

Executive Overview: High-level tracking of Total Revenue ($2.3M), Total Profit ($286K), and Order Volume.

Regional Analysis: Interactive map showing sales distribution across US states with a focus on high-performing hubs like California, Texas, and New York.

Monthly Trends: Visualization of sales seasonality, identifying major peaks in the Q4 holiday season.

Product Insights: Breakdown of sales by sub-category, highlighting "Chairs" and "Phones" as primary revenue drivers.

📈 Data Insights

Seasonality: Sales show significant growth toward the end of the year. The Central region peaks in October, East/South in November, and West in December.

Profitability: Maintained a healthy profit margin across 5,000+ orders.

Regional Hubs: Texas (Central) and California (West) are the most critical states for revenue growth.

🛠️ Tools Used

Power BI Desktop: Data modeling, DAX measures, and Visualization.

Power Query: Data cleaning and transformation.

Excel: Source data format.
